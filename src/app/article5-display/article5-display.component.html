After 2 Injury Plagued Seasons, The Seahawks Have Parted Ways With the Once Promising Duel-Threat Back

Dwayne Johnson
SEAHAWKS.COM REPORTER

SEATTLE, WA. --- The return of Shaun Alexander marks a shift in philosophy and a return to the ground and pound offense that was the heart beat of the Seahawks during their Super Bowl run in 2013. After 2 injury plagued seasons, the Seahawks have parted ways with the once promising duel-threat back, CJ Prosise.
